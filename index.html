About me

Hi

My Name is Ryan Wilson and I am a Photographer

I am an amateur photographer with a passion for capturing the world's unscripted moments through my lens. Born and raised in the beautiful island of Jamaica, I now call London, Ontario Canada home. My journey into photography began during the global pandemic, a a time that absolutely sucked other than things like photography that I am glad I discovered. It did end up opening my eyes to the beauty that surrounds us every day. Equipped with my trusty Olympus EM 10 Mark III, I seek to share my unique perspective and chronicle my journey in photography. Whether you're a fellow amateur photographer or simply a lover of good visuals, I welcome you to join me on this exciting adventure. Let's explore the world one click at a time together!
